## Challenge

Siloed data across 12 business units with no governance framework.
Analysts spent 60% of their time finding and validating data rather than
generating insights. Compliance risk was unquantified.

## Approach

- Partnered with the CDO to design an enterprise data governance framework
- Built a data catalog with lineage tracking, quality scoring, and ownership mapping
- Deployed a semantic layer enabling self-service analytics for 200+ business users
- Created data contracts between producers and consumers with automated enforcement
