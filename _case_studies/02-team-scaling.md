## Challenge

Hyper-growth company needed to scale its data engineering function from a 4-person
team to a fully structured organization supporting 200+ data consumers — without
sacrificing velocity or creating bureaucratic overhead.

## Approach

- Designed a tiered org structure: platform core, domain-embedded engineers, and data reliability
- Created career ladders, IC and management tracks, and competency frameworks
- Established a hiring bar with structured interviews calibrated quarterly
- Implemented team health metrics and engineering effectiveness dashboards
