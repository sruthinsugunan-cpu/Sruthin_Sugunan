Platform Transformation

Real-Time Data Platform Migration

Enterprise SaaS · 2024

$12M Annual Savings

Challenge

A monolithic batch ETL system processing 2TB daily was creating 8-hour data latency, blocking the product team's ability to deliver real-time features and causing downstream ML models to train on stale data.

Approach

  • Led architecture review with stakeholders across Product, ML, and Finance to define SLAs
  • Designed a phased migration strategy — zero downtime, with parallel validation
  • Built a streaming platform on Kafka + Flink with schema evolution support
  • Established data quality gates and automated monitoring from day one

Org Design & Scale

Data Engineering Team from 4 to 40

Series C Fintech · 2022–2024

10× Team Growth

Challenge

Hyper-growth company needed to scale its data engineering function from a 4-person team to a fully structured organization supporting 200+ data consumers — without sacrificing velocity or creating bureaucratic overhead.

Approach

  • Designed a tiered org structure: platform core, domain-embedded engineers, and data reliability
  • Created career ladders, IC and management tracks, and competency frameworks
  • Established a hiring bar with structured interviews calibrated quarterly
  • Implemented team health metrics and engineering effectiveness dashboards

Data Strategy

Unified Data Governance & Self-Service Analytics

Healthcare Technology · 2023

$24M Revenue Enabled

Challenge

Siloed data across 12 business units with no governance framework. Analysts spent 60% of their time finding and validating data rather than generating insights. Compliance risk was unquantified.

Approach

  • Partnered with the CDO to design an enterprise data governance framework
  • Built a data catalog with lineage tracking, quality scoring, and ownership mapping
  • Deployed a semantic layer enabling self-service analytics for 200+ business users
  • Created data contracts between producers and consumers with automated enforcement
